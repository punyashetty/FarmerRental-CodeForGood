About Us

ISAP has one of the largest networks of agriculture and allied sector professionals in India which bridges the network of Indian rural communities with other large stakeholders including governments, large development organizations and corporate entities. This is done through large-scale field implementation projects with integrated deployment for efficiency and scale. They have a wide network across the country with 17 CHSCs. CHCSs are Custom Hiring Service Centers located in remote villages across various states. These centers help the local farmers by lending them modernized agricultural machinery and providing them training in advanced agricultural methods. Data management of these centers is done manually by the center head in hard bound forms/registers. This data is transferred to the supervisors in mails or over WhatsApp. The supervisors visit these CHSCs physically on regular basis. However, this is not very effective way of monitoring. At present, the communication between the headquarters and these centers is done over calls and data is transferred over email as excel sheets. They are looking for a technology solution to help them modernize their approach of managing the centers, improving operational efficiency and optimal utilization of resources. This will help in benefitting maximum possible number of farmers in project region, leading to reduction in drudgery and improved mechanization on the farms. This will eventually have a social impact in increasing income of these farmers.

Mission

Indian Society of Agribusiness Professionals (ISAP) since 2001 is working for the empowerment of rural India through sustainable farm and non-farm livelihood development. Their mission is to enhance livelihood opportunities and thereby result in better quality of life for rural communities through sustainable intensification of Agriculture, Skill Development and Market Integration
